AhnLab Presents Session on Stopping Malware to Protect Digital Assets at IANS Chicago Information Security Forum

Date
10-30-2013
Hit
189

AhnLab MDS Provides Protection From Advanced Persistent Threats Through a Unified and Integrated Approach, Securing Endpoints, Servers, Networks, and the Cloud

SAN FRANCISCO, CA--(Marketwired - Oct 30, 2013) - AhnLab, a leading provider of information security products and services for enterprise business environments, today announced that Leo Versola, vice president of technology, will be a featured presenter at the 2013 IANS Chicago Information Security Forum on November 4-5 at McCormick Place North.


AhnLab's session will focus on preventing malware attacks in a network environment and protecting digital assets from Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs). The presentation will take place during Monday's Technology Spotlight Sessions from 11:05 a.m. - 12:23 p.m. CT.
"With Advanced Persistent Threats, cyber criminals are stealthy and extremely focused, often designing their multi-stage attacks with specific targets in mind," said Leo Versola, vice president of technology, AhnLab. "Traditional security solutions are proving ineffective against these threats, illustrating the importance for C-level executives to take these threats seriously and to reevaluate their current security infrastructures."


Launched in North America earlier this year, AhnLab Malware Defense System (MDS) is the only enterprise strength security system that combines local and cloud-based analytics to stop APTs and zero-day threats anywhere across the organization. AhnLab MDS delivers comprehensive threat protection for endpoints, servers, networks and cloud resources, all within a single architecture and integrated management environment.


Benefits and features of AhnLab MDS include:
• Simplified management and rapid response to APTs anywhere across the enterprise
• Automated malware removal and targeted disruption of malware network activity without affecting normal business operations
• Scanning of popular file formats frequently used by APTs for propagation in real-time
• More user-friendly log search fields and advanced search options, with wildcard search feature
• Enhanced file analysis and risk alert for detection events
For more information on AhnLab MDS, please visit www.ahnlab.com.

 

About the Chicago Information Security Forum and IANS
The Chicago Information Security Forum brings together experienced IT and information security practitioners for confidential information sharing on the industry's most important issues, technologies, and trends. The two-day Forum includes keynote addresses, technical and strategic roundtable discussions led by IANS' Faculty, networking events, and the opportunity to learn about new technologies.


IANS is the leading provider of in-depth security insights and decision support delivered through research, community, and consulting. Fueled by interactions among IANS Faculty and information security practitioners, IANS' experience-driven advice helps IT security, risk management, and compliance executives make better, faster technical and managerial decisions.
IANS was founded in 2001 as the Institute for Applied Network Security. Inspired by the Harvard Business School experience of interactive discussions driving collective insights, IANS adapted that format to fit the needs of the information security community.
