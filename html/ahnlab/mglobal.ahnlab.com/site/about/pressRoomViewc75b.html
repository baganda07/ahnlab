AhnLab introduces its Anti-APT Security Solutions at CommunicAsia 2014

Date
06-18-2014
Hit
323

Seoul, June 18, 2014 — AhnLab, Inc. (www.ahnlab.com), a leading provider of information security products and services for enterprise, military and government organizations, and Synetcom Philippines, Inc., AhnLab’s strategic business partner, introduced AhnLab MDS, multi-layered security solutions against today’s advanced security threats at the CommunicAsia 2014 (Booth Number BB5-08, Basement Level), held on June 17-20, 2014 at Marina Bay Sands, Singapore.

 

CommunicAsia 2014 is known to be the one of the largest and the most important event recognized by industry professionals in Singapore, continues to strengthen and stay relevant to the ever changing info-communications technology industry.

In this event, AhnLab introduced AhnLab MDS (Malware Defense System), the corporate security solution which combines local and cloud-based analytics to cope with APTs (Advanced Persistent Threats) across the organization.

 

“We’re pleased to take part in this big exhibition to share the latest security information and technology with the security experts”, said Chi-Jung Kwon, CEO of AhnLab. “As the security threats are getting more complex and comprehensive, AhnLab will continue to introduce innovative security solution and services to give users peace of mind”

 

AhnLab MDS (Malware Defense System)
AhnLab MDS delivers comprehensive threat protection, combining cloud-based security intelligence, multi-dimensional behavior based malware analysis and its patented DICA (Dynamic Intelligent Content Analysis) technology.

It detects known malware with its ASD, AhnLab Smart Defense, the cloud-based security intelligence system, while utilize multi-dimensional behavior based malware analysis technology to detect unknown malware and/or its variants. It not only examines behavior of the unknown file in the network with its embedded sandbox, but analyzes additional information including malicious characteristics, the risk level of the URLs or IP addresses that the file connects to, reputation information, and comprehensive behavior patterns.

 

The solution also analyzes non-executable files (non-PE), including .doc and .pdf, by adopting patented “DICA (Dynamic Intelligent Content Analysis)” Technology. With this technology, the security administrators of the organizations can detect malicious shell codes exploits vulnerabilities even before it actually delivers malicious activities. It also provides an immediate response including remediation or blocking the execution of the unknown files to prevent potential threat.

 

AhnLab MDS is the only solution that delivers comprehensive host and network protection against known and unknown malware, zero-day exploits, and advanced persistent threats.

 

Meanwhile, for several years, AhnLab has been worked closely with many global companies to expand partnerships and distribution channels in the Global Markets including APAC market. Also, AhnLab launched the Technical Workshop at Singapore last month for partners in APAC. The attendees were being given AhnLab’s industry-leading security technology about MDS and DPS (DDoS attack Protection System) and had got ACSA (AhnLab Certified Security Architect) certificate.

 

 

About AhnLab, Inc.
AhnLab creates agile, integrated Internet security solutions for consumers and businesses, ranging in size from SMBs to enterprise organizations. Founded in 1995, AhnLab is a global leader in security research and product development that delivers comprehensive protection for networks, transactions, and essential services. By combining cloud analysis with endpoint and server resources, AhnLab generates best-of-breed breach detection and threat prevention that scales easily for large and high-speed networks. This multi-dimensional approach combines with exceptional service to create truly global protection against attacks that evade traditional security defenses.
