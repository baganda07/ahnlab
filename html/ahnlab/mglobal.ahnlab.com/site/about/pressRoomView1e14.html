Frost & Sullivan Recognizes AhnLab with the 2015 South Korea Technology Innovation Award in APT Solutions

Date
09-21-2015
Hit
1524

SEOUL, Sept 17, 2015 – Frost & Sullivan has recognized AhnLab Inc with the 2015 South Korea Technology Innovation Award in Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) solutions.


Mr. Charles Lim, Senior Industry Analyst, Networking, Information and Cyber Security, Asia Pacific at Frost & Sullivan said that AhnLab has differentiated itself from other security vendors in its drive for excellence in developing a holistic APT solution in South Korea.


“In its latest MDS – Malware Defense System lineup, AhnLab has developed several proprietary algorithms to analyze advanced malware and various attack vectors across the network, providing the right approach to mitigate threats and perform remediation as a total solution,” he added.


He said that AhnLab uses a hybrid technology of static analysis and dynamic analysis to detect malware with its automated analysis technology at the assembly-level. With this, it is capable of blocking and analyzing evasive malware or variants that infiltrate into the endpoint via various ways, such as encrypted traffic using SSL, a USB drive or though the trusted internal network.


He added that due to its product efficiencies, the industry views AhnLab as the top 2 vendors in South Korea, which also puts the company within the top 10 APT vendors in Asia Pacific.


“The MDS’ ease of use, clear visibility of attack vectors and accuracy of detection were well-received by key IT security professionals in the region,” Mr. Lim said.

 


 


AhnLab Vice President, Mr. Suk-kyoon Kang said the company is honored to be recognized with the 2015 South Korea Technology Innovation Award in APT Solutions by Frost & Sullivan, a reputable consulting firm.


“I would like to dedicate the award to all our employees for their hard work. It really motivates us to continue to provide all our clients with the best APT solutions,” he said.


The Frost & Sullivan South Korea Excellence awards, in its fourth year running now, have identified and honored best-in-class companies that have demonstrated excellence in their respective industries.

 

The recipients of the 2015 Frost & Sullivan South Korea Excellence Awards were identified based on an in-depth research conducted by Frost & Sullivan's analysts. The award categories offered each year are carefully reviewed and evaluated to reflect the current market landscape and include new emerging trends.

 

The short-listed companies were evaluated on a variety of actual market performance indicators which include revenue growth; market share and growth in market share; leadership in product innovation; marketing strategy and business development strategy.
